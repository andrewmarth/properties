Back-due Property Tax and HOA Fees: All properties are taxed by the county and some properties are located within homeowner's associations. Although our software makes a "call" to the county treasurer, there are times when the data is not up to date. If you have a property in mind, it is best to go to http://treasurer.maricopa.gov/ to confirm the amount of property tax owed (if any). The purchaser becomes responsible for any unpaid property tax. Likewise, the purchaser may be responsible, for a portion of existing homeowners association fees. Also, it is typical that homeowners associations will require a transfer fee from the new owner (you).
